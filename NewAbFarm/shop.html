Abbington Farm Shop

The farm shop has a wide range of fresh produce including milk, yoghurt & cheese, jams & preserves and a wide variety of bread & cakes. Our free-range eggs are laid by the chickens on our farm and our pork is reared in the surrounding fields, giving it a distinctive flavour.

Choose from award winning pies with fillings such as: apricot or blackberry; hand-made chocolates; local honey and marmalade; homemade breads and cakes; locally sourced fruit juices, wines, beers and ciders. We also sell fresh vegetables grown in our garden.
