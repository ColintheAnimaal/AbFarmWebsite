Abbington Farm

Our petting zoo contains all widely known domesticated animals including cows, sheep, goats, pigs, chickens, horses, geese and even an alpaca. Some of the activities we offer are hayrides, pumpkin picking, and a corn maze.

Visit the animals and see how they live in their natural habitat, view farm machinery and play with farm tools such as a pitchfork or shovel, or pick your own produce from the garden to take home with you.
