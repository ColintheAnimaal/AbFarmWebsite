Local Area

Abington was strategically important for the Romans and there is still evidence of forts and settlements in the area. Just north of the village are earthworks that date from the 12th century. The local countryside has some amazing hills for going walking.

In the heart of the village is the Upper Clyde Parish Church, a grey and brown stone building edged in red whose foundation stone was laid on 9th August 1898.
